Beri aad u fog waxa saaxiib ahaa Dhurwaa iyo Dawaco. Dhurwaaga waxa la odhan jirey **Dhuruq**, Dawacadana **Dayo**. Meel bay ku wada noolaayeen. Berigaas, habar‑dugaaggu wuxuu dhisan jirey aqallo la mid ah kuwa bini‑aadmiga. Dhuruq wuxuu lahaa cariish aad u fiican oo lahaa albaab iyo daaqad, Dayona waxay lahayd waab xun oo meel walba ka daldaloolay.

Dayo iyo Dhuruq waxay ku wada heshiiyeen in mid walba wixii uu helo uu kan kale wax ka siiyo. Dayo waxay ka badnayd Dhuruq siyaasad iyo xeelad, wixii ay hesho way ka qarsan jirtay. Dhuruq se waxba kama qarsan jirin; wixii uu helo wuu keeni jirey, wayna wada cuni jireen.

Maalin ayaa roob weyn da’ay. Onkod iyo hillaac ayaa cirka buuxiyey. Dayo welwel baa galay, maxaa yeelay waabkeedii xumaa ma celin karayn roobka. Markaasay ku fikirtay sidii ay Dhuruq uga khiyaamayn lahayd cariishkiisa fiican.

Waxay u tagtay Dhuruq oo tidhi:

> “Dhurugow, roob baa meel walba ka da’aya, cadowgeenuna waa badan yahay. Haddii roobku yimaado oo cadowgu kadinka istaago, xaggee baad ka baxaysaa? Cariishkaagu meel kale ma laha. Aniguna marka lays i soo hortaago, meel walba baan ka baxayaa.”

Dhuruq ayaa hadalkaas rumaystay, wuuna baqay. Wuxuu yidhi:

> “Dayooy, waa runtaa. Meel aan ka baxo ma lihi. Bal caawa gurigaaga aan galo, adiguna cariishkayga gal.”

Dayo markii hore way is diidday, balse markuu baryay ayay yeelatay, iyada oo qalbiga ku faraxsan. Sidaasay ku khiyaamaysay Dhuruq, waabkeediina u beddeshay, ayaduna cariishkii Dhuruq gashay.

Habeenkii roobkii ayaa aad u da’ay. Waabkii Dayo ma celin karin dhibic. Dhuruq wuu wada qoqobay. Marba meel ayuu ka boodayay, onkodka iyo hillaaca ayuu mooday colkii Dayo sheegtay. Ugu dambayn, isagoo daallan, ayuu ku noqday waabka, Dayona albaabka ayay hoosta ka xidhatay oo si nabad ah u seexatay.

Roobkii wuu sii watay. Dhuruq qabow iyo dhaxan ayuu la gariiray. Albaabka ayuu ku garaacay:

> “Dayooy, albaabka iga fur!”

Dayo way maqashay, balse iska indho‑tiray. Markii waagu beryay ayay albaabka furtay. Dhuruq wuu wada qoyay, dhaxan baa gawsihiisii isugu dhacayay.

Wuxuu yidhi:

> “Haddii aan xalay kugu garaacayay albaabka, maxaad ii furi weyday?”

Dayo ayaa ku qososhay oo tidhi:

> “Alla! Albaabku wuu furnaa. Maxaad u soo gelin weyday? Habeenka oo dhan waan ku lahaa: ‘Dhurugow, roobka ka soo gal!’”

Markii uu yara diiray, Dayo waxay tidhi:

> “Gaajo weyn baa jirta. Bal ina keen meskaraddii ina aadno.”

Way isla tageen. Intii ay socdaan ayay arkeen adhi meel ku xareysan. Dayo waxay tidhi:

> “Anigu xoog ma lihi. Adigu geli, wan weyn noo soo xulo. Aniga irridda ayaan ka joogayaa; haddii dad yimaadaan waan ku qaylinayaa.”

Dhuruq ayaa xero galay. Isla markiiba raggii adhiga lahaa ayaa ka soo noqday suuqii. Dayo way aragtay, balse kama qaylin. Iyadaa baxsatay.

Dhuruq isagoo wan tuur saaran ayuu irridda ku soo baxay, raggii ayaa ku qayliyey. Wankii ayuu tuuray, raggii ayuu dhex maray, balse nin ayaa budh kaga dhuftay lug dambe. Wuu baxsaday, isagoo dhutinaya.

Markii uu Dayo gaaray ayuu yidhi:

> “Maxaad iigu qaylin weyday?”

Waxay ugu jawaabtay:

> “Haddii aad dhego beeshay, aniga miyaa kuu samaynaya? Waan ku qayliyay ee adiga ayaa mashquulnaa.”

Lugtii Dhuruq way barartay, akhirnya wuu naafoobay.

Maalin Dayo waxay dhacday neef. Badh bay cuntay, badhna way qarsatay. Reerkii neefka lahaa dabin bay dhigeen. Maalin dambe Dayo waxay u timid hilibkii la qarsaday, dabinkii bay gashay, mana bixi karin.

Dhuruq ayaa maqnaanteeda raadiyey ilaa uu ku arkay dabinka dhexdiisa. Dayo waxay ku tidhi:

> “Reer abtigay baa i martiqaaday. Halkan soo gal.”

Dhuruq intuu ka shakiyey ayuu arkay dad middiyo sita oo soo qaylinaya. Markaas ayuu garatay khiyaamadeedii, wuxuuna yidhi:

> “Wax badan baad i sirtay, balse daacadnimadaydu way i badbaadisay. Waxaa la yidhi: *daacadnimo ma hungowdo*.”

Wuu cararay. Dayona halkaas ayaa lagu dilay.

**DHAMMAAD**
